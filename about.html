Biography

Catie Riel is a Filipina-American Design student at the University of San Francisco. She is also minoring in UX/UI design and plans to graduate in Spring 2027.

Having lived in a cities of diverse economic, social, and ethnic backgrounds, Catie holds a strong sense of social justice. She aims for creative solutions with interdisciplinary collaboration. Outside of design, you can find her at the beach, on a hike, or in the pit of a metal concert.
