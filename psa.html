PSA Poster: Obsessive Compulsive Disorder

This poster was an assignment for a Visual Communication class. The assignment required a topic of focus and include a tagline. As a person who suffers from OCD, Catie chose the disorder as her topic. The poster includes drawn handcuffs, designed to spell "OCD." The background features ragged and chaoticly-placed text.

Made in Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop
